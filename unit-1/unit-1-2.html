CEFR descriptors for the C1 level

Read the next file and as you go over the descriptors, reflect on your own language proficiency.

CERF descriptors for C1

Having taken a practice FCE exam, you are now familiar with the format of Cambridge English tests: just as FCE, a Cambridge English: Advanced Exam (CAE before 2015) inclused papers on use of English, reading, writing, listening and speaking. Since changes made in 2015, the use of English and reading papers have been combined. You can find out more about the exam format.

  • C1 Advanced exam format.

You may also be interested in looking at the Cambridge English: Advanced handbook for teachers.

Cambridge English Qualifications

The first section (p. 2-6, Cambridge English Qualifications) provides a general introduction to the exam, while the later sections look at the different papers in more detail.

For the purpose of this course, we will look at use of English and reading separately, as they can be considered as different aspects of language.
