1.6 Grammar. Dependent prepositions

By this, I’m thinking ________ verbs like “think” that take a specific preposition that goes into that space. Do you know which one it is?

Prepositions are tricky, they change from one language to the next and the logic behind them is hard to decipher. The only way is to learn them and practice.

Example:

I’m thinking ___ joining a gym this year.
I’ve been thinking ___ it all morning.
It depends ___ whether I have enough money or not.

Can you fill in the gaps?
